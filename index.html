Handcrafted

Dorff furniture is handcrafted from white oak and walnut. It’s made to last, and built on the principle that what’s made slow and built by hand is done right. We use old world techniques paired with modern machinery to make pieces that will bridge generations.

Made in Encinitas

Every piece of furniture created by DORFF is designed and made in Encinitas, California. From the first drawing to the finishing touches, every step takes place in a shitty garage converted into a modern wood shop. Inspired by world travels, while made at home.
